About Thunderbird

  • Thunderbird is located near Highway 285 and 380 in Roswell, New Mexico. Our community is perfect for families and individuals looking for a safe and comfortable place to call home.

  • Thunderbird MHP strikes a perfect balance between accessibility and personal space. Our park is located in close proximity to major retailers like Target, Walmart, and Albertons and several recreation and open space areas like the Spring River Zoo.

  • The city of Roswell provides access to over 20 parks and recreation areas. This includes walking and biking trails, baseball and softball complexes, and a state park that provides opportunities to fish and camp.

  • We are a pet-friendly community and ask for a refundable deposit of $100. All pets must be approved by management prior to the move in date due to insurance requirements. There is a limit as to how many pets a household may have.

  • If you're interested in joining our community, we ask that you fill out an application form, which you can find by clicking the Application tab above. Our friendly management team is available to answer any questions you may have.

  • Thanks for your interest and we look forward to your visit.
